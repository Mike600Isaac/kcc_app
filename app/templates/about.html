About Kindness Church

A vibrant family of believers growing in grace and burning for God

Our History

Kindness Church began as a humble fellowship of believers gathered in the parlour of a devoted member. What started as a small assembly united by faith, prayer, and a deep hunger for God steadily grew through divine grace and dedication.

From those modest beginnings, the church flourished into a location church and has now grown into a district — a testament to God’s faithfulness and the commitment of zealous believers. Rooted firmly in the teaching of God’s Word, holiness, and fervent prayer, the church continues to expand in vision and impact.

Today, Kindness Church stands at yet another remarkable milestone. With the ongoing development of Kindness Tower, the church embraces a new season of growth, purpose, and spiritual influence — reaching hearts across all walks of life, including the great and elite of the land.
